FAQ

Here is a list of answers to frequently asked questions about the technical fest.

  • 01. General FAQ
Where will all these events be hosted?

All the above mentioned events will be hosted in the campus premises of Cambridge International School, Amritsar.

I am not a student of Cambridge. Can I still participate?

Yes. The event is not limited to the students of any particular school. Any student can participate. However, the registration is to be done by the school on the behalf of its students.

Can I register independently as a student?

We expect your school to register the team. In case you register independently, we still require your school to endorse you.

Can I participate in more than one event?

No. All the events will be conducted simultaneously. So, you cannot participate in more than one event.
